The Industry Challenge

Lack of Connections

3 out of 4 creative industry jobs go to friends and existing connections, leaving graduates without networks at a disadvantage.

Hidden Opportunities

Many film industry positions are never publicly advertised - they're filled through word of mouth.

Experience Gap

Graduates have the skills but lack the industry insider knowledge and vouching needed to land their first role.

Our Solution: Professional Mentorship

Connecting passionate graduates with experienced professionals who remember what it's like to start out
